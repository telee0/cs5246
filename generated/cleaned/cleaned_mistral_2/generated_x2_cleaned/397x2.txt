

1) Labour MPs have been warned to brace themselves for a snap general election as soon as next week, according to sources close to Jeremy Corbin. One source confirmed that talks were underway to hold an early poll, and said the party was preparing accordingly. The leader of the opposition told BBC Radio Five Live: “We are working hard every day.”
2) Boris Johnson has indicated that an agreement on Brexit is unlikely before October, meaning Britain could face another no-deal departure from the EU. This came after Theresa May’s government suffered yet another defeat in Parliament over her deal. The prime minister told BBC Radio Five Live: “I think it’s important we don’t make any assumptions about what might happen.”
