In an interview with CBC News, Bishop Michael Miller expressed concern for families who were forced to leave their homes due to violence. He highlighted the need for greater investment in education, job training programs, and affordable housing initiatives. In addition, he called for stronger efforts to combat corruption within government agencies responsible for addressing social issues. While acknowledging that there is much work left to be done, he emphasized the importance of working together towards common goals and finding sustainable solutions that benefit all members of society.
