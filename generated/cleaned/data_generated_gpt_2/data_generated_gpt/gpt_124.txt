First Minister orders report on decision to allow unguarded visit for paranoid schizophrenic knife attacker
First Minister Jack McConnell has mandated a report to be conducted regarding the decision to permit a paranoid schizophrenic knife attacker to go on a visit unguarded. The individual in question, Michael Ferguson, aged 36, managed to escape after being granted permission to leave the high-security Carstairs unit. This incident has sparked concern and raised questions about the protocols and procedures in place for monitoring and managing individuals with severe mental health issues who pose a risk to themselves and others.
