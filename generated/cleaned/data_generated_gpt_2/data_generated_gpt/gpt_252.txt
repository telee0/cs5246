Labour and Conservatives continue campaign phone calls amid regulations
Labour and the Conservatives are continuing to reach out to the millions of individuals who have registered to avoid receiving unwanted marketing calls to ensure compliance with regulations. Both parties have stated that they are able to abide by the rules by making sure their calls are not classified as marketing, such as by inquiring about individuals' voting preferences. The ongoing efforts to engage with voters through phone calls highlight the importance of adhering to regulations while still actively campaigning for support.
