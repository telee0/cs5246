Sandra Howard to Take on Prominent Role in Conservative Election Campaign
Michael Howard's former model wife, Sandra, is set to have a significant presence in the upcoming Conservative election campaign. Mrs. Howard will be conducting independent visits to key constituencies, in addition to joining her husband on his busy campaign schedule which includes traveling by helicopter to various locations across the country. This move marks a strategic decision by the Conservative party to utilize Mrs. Howard's public profile and charisma to engage with voters and support the party's efforts to secure victory in the election.
