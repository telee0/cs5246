Tory Leader Michael Howard Vows to Slash Government Spending by £35bn
Conservative leader Michael Howard has pledged to cut government spending by £35 billion by targeting wasteful expenditures. He plans to redirect the saved funds towards improving essential services such as the NHS and schools, while also using a portion to decrease government borrowing and lower taxes. Howard believes that by addressing inefficiencies and unnecessary expenses, his party can significantly reduce the financial burden on the government and provide better services for the public.
