Lib Dem leader Charles Kennedy criticizes Tony Blair's lack of trustworthiness
Lib Dem leader Charles Kennedy has expressed concerns over the lack of trust voters have in Prime Minister Tony Blair. Kennedy emphasized that supporting the Liberal Democrats is not a wasted vote, highlighting the party's transparency and consistency. According to Kennedy, the Lib Dems offer honesty and integrity, contrasting with what he perceives as a lack of trustworthiness in the current government. He urged voters to consider the values and principles of his party when casting their ballots, believing that the Lib Dems offer a genuine and trustworthy alternative to the current political landscape.
