Ministers Stand by Free Personal Care for Elderly Despite Underestimation of Costs
Government officials have reiterated their dedication to providing free personal care for the elderly, despite recent findings indicating that the costs of the policy were not accurately estimated. According to a report from the Fraser of Allander Institute, the decision to move forward with the prominent policy was made on the basis of faulty research. Despite this revelation, ministers have maintained their commitment to ensuring that elderly individuals receive the necessary care and support they deserve.
