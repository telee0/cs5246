Prince Charles and Camilla Parker Bowles to Marry in Civil Ceremony at Windsor Castle
Prince Charles and Camilla Parker Bowles have recently made headlines with their announcement of their upcoming marriage on 8 April. The nation eagerly awaits their reaction to this news. Following the civil ceremony at Windsor Castle, Mrs Parker Bowles will be known as HRH Duchess of Cornwall. This royal union is sure to be a significant event in the history of the British monarchy.
