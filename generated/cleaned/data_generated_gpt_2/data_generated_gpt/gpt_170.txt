Michael Howard Defends £4bn Tax Cut Plans Against Conservative Criticism
Conservative leader Michael Howard has brushed off concerns from within his party that his proposed £4bn tax cuts are too conservative. In response to critics, Howard stated that the tax cut package is just the beginning and that he is hopeful for the opportunity to make additional cuts in the future. The Tory leader emphasized that the proposed tax cuts are part of the party's initial budget plan and that he is committed to potentially expanding the cuts in the future.
