Science Minister Lord Sainsbury Makes £2m Donation to Labour Party for General Election Fund
The Labour Party has received a substantial boost in funding for its General Election campaign, with Science Minister Lord Sainsbury making a generous £2m donation. This contribution is just the latest in a series of large donations made by the billionaire, who has already contributed over £10m to the party since 1999. Lord Sainsbury's financial support is likely to have a significant impact on the party's election efforts, providing crucial funding for campaigning and outreach activities.
