Former Labour Prime Minister Lord Callaghan Passes Away at the Age of 92
The former Labour prime minister, Lord Callaghan, has sadly passed away at the age of 92 on the eve of his 93rd birthday. He served as prime minister from 1976 to 1979, leading the country through a challenging period in its history. Lord Callaghan was known for his strong leadership and his commitment to social justice. He will be remembered for his dedication to public service and his contributions to the Labour Party.
