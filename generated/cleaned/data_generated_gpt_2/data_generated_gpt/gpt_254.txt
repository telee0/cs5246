Robert Kilroy-Silk Launches New Party to 'Change the Face of British Politics'
Former BBC chat show host and East Midlands MEP Robert Kilroy-Silk has announced the creation of a new party in an effort to alter the landscape of British politics. Kilroy-Silk, who recently departed from the UK Independence Party, expressed his concerns about the impact of mass immigration on the nation, stating that 'our country' is being taken from its citizens. The politician is determined to make a difference and bring attention to what he sees as a critical issue facing the UK.
